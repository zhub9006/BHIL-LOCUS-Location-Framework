# P09: Business Environment

## Summary
**Score: 5.0/10** — Mixed; strong restaurant/retail but limited commercial office space

### Commercial Activity
- **NW 23rd Avenue:** Major commercial corridor with dense restaurant and retail
- **Nob Hill Shopping District:** Independent boutiques and specialty shops
- **Restaurant Density:** 89 within 1km — among Portland's highest concentrations

### Nearby Employment Centers
- **Downtown Portland:** ~2-3km (tech, finance, government)
- **Pearl District:** ~2km (creative, tech)
- **NW Industrial:** ~5km (distribution, logistics)

### Business Services
- Co-working spaces accessible in adjacent neighborhoods
- Creative office space in Pearl District
- Small business ecosystem strong in Nob Hill corridor

### Economic Indicators
- Commercial vacancy: Low in prime NW 23rd Ave locations
- Retail rent: Premium pricing
- Foot traffic: High, especially on weekends
