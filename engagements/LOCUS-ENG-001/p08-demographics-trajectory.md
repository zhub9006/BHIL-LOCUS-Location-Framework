# P08: Demographic Character & Trajectory

## Neighborhood: Nob Hill / Northwest District, Portland

### General Character
- **Established:** Early 20th century residential neighborhood, gentrified and revitalized over the past 20 years
- **Housing Mix:** Pre-war apartments, vintage homes, new mixed-use developments
- **Median Household Income:** Above Portland average (estimated $80K-$100K)
- **Education Level:** High — college-educated population majority
- **Age Mix:** Predominantly 25-45 (young professionals, couples), with some families and retirees

### Demographic Trajectory
- **Trend:** Increasingly desirable, prices rising
- **Development:** Active infill development along NW 23rd Avenue corridor
- **Commercial Growth:** New restaurants, boutiques, and cafes continue to open
- **Transit-Oriented Development:** Benefiting from proximity to future MAX alignments

### Neighborhood Vibe
- Arts and culture-forward
- Strong LGBTQ+ community presence
- Eclectic mix of vintage and modern
- Dog-friendly, bike-friendly
- Active neighborhood association (Nob Hill Association)

### Census Context
- Population density: High urban density
- Walk Score trend: Consistently among Portland's highest
- Housing: Mix of rental and ownership; rents above city average
