# P06: Transportation & Commute

## Summary
**Score: 9.4/10** — Excellent multi-modal transportation access

### Public Transit
- **TriMet Bus Lines:** Multiple lines serve NW 23rd Avenue
  - Line 4 (NW 23rd/21st) — connects to downtown and SE Portland
  - Line 44 (NW Lovejoy) — connects to NW Industrial and downtown
  - Line 16 (NW Thurman) — connects to NW Portland neighborhoods
- **MAX Light Rail:** Accessible within ~15 min walk (NW 23rd & Davis or NW Johnson stations)

### Transit Score
- Excellent bus connectivity
- Light rail accessible within 15 min walk
- Portland's transit map is well-integrated

### Cycling Infrastructure
- **Bike Score:** Excellent — Portland is one of America's most bike-friendly cities
- Neighborhood Greenways on adjacent streets
- Bike lanes on NW 23rd Avenue

### Walkability
- **Walk Score: 10/10** — Walker's Paradise
- No car needed for daily life
- Ride-share (Uber/Lyft) readily available

### Airport Access
Portland International Airport (PDX) is approximately 20 min by car/taxi, 45 min by TriMet MAX + bus connection.
