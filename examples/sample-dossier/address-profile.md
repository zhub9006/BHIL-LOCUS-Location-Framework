## Neighborhood Character

Capitol Hill is Seattle's densest and most walkable neighborhood, anchored by
the Pike/Pine corridor. It serves as the city's primary arts, LGBTQ+, and
nightlife district. The neighborhood is characterized by mixed-use density,
abundant street-level retail, and strong transit connectivity via Link Light Rail
(Capitol Hill Station, 0.3 mi).

Land use in the immediate 0.25 mi radius is predominantly commercial/mixed-use
ground floor with residential above. Single-family parcels are minimal; the area
is zoned NC3P-85 (Neighborhood Commercial with pedestrian overlay, 85-ft height
limit).
